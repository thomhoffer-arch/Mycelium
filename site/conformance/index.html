conformance

Prove your connector fits.

Conformance is the trust signal. A connector earns it by passing a small, zero-dependency kit: a validator, a sample, and the checklist below. Pass it, register in the hub, carry the verified badge.

The kit

Three files. No build, no dependencies. Run it locally or in CI.

# validate one sample
node conformance/validate.mjs conformance/sample.json

# self-certify your connector (see the reference)
node connectors/reference-connector/connector.mjs

The validator returns either { conformant: true } or a list of specific errors. Wire it into your CI so a regression can't ship.

Maturity badge

  • experimental — passes the kit on a fixture; not battle-tested.
  • community — in real use somewhere; not independently verified.
  • verified — passes the kit and has been tested on a real project. Prefer this in production.
