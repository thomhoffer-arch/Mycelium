Connective spine

A tiny, versioned contract every source conforms to: a stable identity, a freshness stamp, and a hash-chained provenance event. Join on opaque keys — no human names required.

Connectors

Any source — Revit, ArchiCAD, Rhino, ERPNext, BCF, email — becomes spine records over MCP, REST or files. Each connector is its own open repo; the hub indexes them.

Conformance

A zero-dependency kit proves your connector fits the contract before it ships. Pass it, register in the hub, carry the verified badge.
